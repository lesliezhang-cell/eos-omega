FAQ

Questions, answered

What does "officially recognized" mean?

A sanctioned EOS Network partner — direct access to core teams, grants and infrastructure for our founders.

Who should apply?

Teams building apps, infrastructure or capital markets on EOS — from idea to live traction.

Do you take equity or tokens?

Varies by track. Flagship is a standard investment; incubation is grant-based. Specifics come at the offer stage.

Is the program remote or in person?

Remote-first, with optional summits and an in-person Demo Day. Apply from anywhere.

When does the next cohort start?

Rolling review, quarterly cohorts. Apply any time — strong teams are fast-tracked.
